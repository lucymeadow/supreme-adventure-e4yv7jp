Mechanism glossary

  • Token — Semantic colour and type tokens (and sometimes mark variant selection via theme) from EGDS and DTS.
  • CDN — Binary mark from the EGDS or brand CDN path, or another approved CDN URL.
  • Travelstore — Content and layout authoring for traveler-facing pages and some email placements (Q1 hub).
  • Manual — Hand-edited SVG or PNG, email HTML, app binary, partner CMS, or hardcoded path outside token-only updates.
  • DNS — Mostly BIMI and similar inbox brand indicators, not the main site logo.
  • DMS — Dynamic messaging or campaign surfaces configured outside the core EGDS nav path (per issue tracker examples).
