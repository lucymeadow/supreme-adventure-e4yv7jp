Priority 7 vs Other Top 10 Overlap Audit

Portfolio research published 2026-04-09. Sources: Priority Story and Milestone Map per strategy; P7 objective map synced from Jira 2026-04-07; other nine Top 10 maps synced 2026-04-09.

Audit of Make Hotels.com the Most Rewarding Way to Book a Hotel (STRAT1-1346, Priority 7) against the other nine EG Top 10 priorities. Goal: where Priority 7 story themes and milestones overlap other priorities’ stories and Jira milestone inventories.

Update (2026-04-09)

Priority 7 tracks Hotels.com dedicated servicing under MLSTN1-5883 (Provide Dedicated Service Solutions). MLSTN1-5681 is parented under Win the Whole Trip on BEX for BEX family lodging. This audit treats 5883 as the HCOM milestone and 5681 as the BEX-side sibling.

Methodology

  • Read Priority Story and Milestone Map for Priority 7 in the product-portfolio repo.
  • Read each other strategy’s Milestone Map (flat list from Jira) and cross-checked priority stories where themes recurred.
  • Classified overlap as story (narrative / dependency alignment), milestone (same or parallel Jira work), or platform (shared capability, single owner).

How to read this

  • Story overlap — Same customer problems, capabilities, or dependencies in narrative. Not always the same Jira parent.
  • Milestone overlap — Same workstream, mirrored pilots, or one issue appearing under two strategies. Strongest signal when governance is unclear.
  • Platform overlap — Intentional: P7 depends on identity, payments, loyalty ledger, OMS, i18n, marketplace foundations. One owner, many consumers.

Synthesis

Yes — a meaningful slice of Priority 7 is also represented elsewhere, in two ways:

  1. By design (shared platform): Identity, payments, loyalty ledger, EG Profile/CDP, merchandising/DLP, NL search, price systems, international rollout. The P7 story lists these teams explicitly.
  2. By potential duplication or split ownership (needs GC clarity):
    • 5883 (HCOM) vs 5681 (BEX) for dedicated service naming/parentage.
    • Dual CFAR pilots (Hcom vs BEX).
    • One Key / Hotels.comCash / VIPa vs BEX One Key evolution and Vrbo OneKey.
    • AI compare / property expert / NL HCOM under P1 and P3 as well as in the P7 story.

Recommendations

  1. Jira hygiene: Keep child initiatives and reporting for HCOM servicing under MLSTN1-5883; use MLSTN1-5681 for BEX family lodging only. Reconcile any stragglers parented to the wrong key.
  2. Capability matrix: For each P7 objective, tag milestones as HCOM-only, Shared platform (single owner), or Parallel brand pilot (explicit dual track).
  3. Refresh: Re-run a flat parent = STRAT1-1346 export periodically and align the objective-grouped Milestone Map table in the repo.

Open questions

  • Should 5883 and 5681 share a single executive-facing narrative slide, or stay strictly separated by brand?
  • For CFAR and One Key, where does GC want a single decision forum vs allowed brand divergence?
