Obj 3
Use rewards to bridge business and leisure travel and create rewards flywheel
Customer Problems
1
Travelers don't feel meaningfully rewarded. Loyalty value is unclear, slow to unlock, or too small to influence behavior — disconnected from how they travel across business and leisure.
2
High-frequency US and UK business travelers miss the 10-for-1 value prop and don't find OneKey earn rates meaningful.
3
Tier status benefits don't feel meaningful, clearly differentiated, or reliably delivered — inconsistency erodes status credibility.
